Privacy Policy for FabVision AI

Last updated: May 4, 2026

FabVision AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is operated by OctoDo Solutions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Phone number (verified via OTP)
  • Email address (optional)
  • Business name and city
  • Garment types you manufacture

Content You Create

  • Fabric photos you upload or capture
  • AI-generated images and videos
  • Fabric classifications and metadata

Device Permissions

  • Camera: To capture fabric photos for AI generation
  • Photo Library/Gallery: To select existing fabric images
  • Storage: To save generated content to your device
  • Internet: To upload fabrics and download AI-generated content

Usage Data

  • App interactions, feature usage, error logs
  • Generation history
  • Credit balance and transaction history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI image and video generation services
  • Process payments and manage credit balance
  • Authenticate your account via Firebase Auth
  • Send generation completion notifications
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Provide customer support via WhatsApp
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data stored on AWS (Amazon Web Services) servers
  • Images and videos stored in encrypted AWS S3 buckets
  • Authentication uses Firebase (Google Cloud)
  • Industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfer
  • Authentication tokens are securely hashed

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) - User login
  • AWS S3 (Amazon) - Image/video storage
  • Google Gemini API - AI fabric classification and image generation
  • Google Veo API - AI video generation
  • Razorpay (planned) - Payment processing

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your fabric photos and generated content are private to your account.

We may share data only when:

  • Required by law or court order
  • Necessary to operate the service (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud)
  • You explicitly consent

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your generated content
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while account is active
  • Generated content: Retained until you delete it
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years (Indian tax law)

8. Children's Privacy

FabVision AI is intended for business users (textile manufacturers) and is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. AI-Generated Content

Content generated by FabVision AI uses Google's Veo and Gemini AI models. Generated videos may include Google's SynthID watermark and C2PA metadata indicating AI generation, in compliance with Google's policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
